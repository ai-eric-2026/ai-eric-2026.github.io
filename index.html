Generative AI for Materials Discovery

肖航课题组开发了首个基于字符串的可逆晶体表示法：SLICES，实现了生成式AI驱动的 Text2Crystal。SLICES有望成为AI驱动的晶体材料逆向设计的标准工具，即晶体材料的 "SMILES"。

The XIAO Research Group developed the first string-based invertible, invariant crystallographic representation: SLICES, achieving Text2Crystal powered by generative AI. SLICES has the potential to become a standard tool for in-silico materials discovery, i.e., "SMILES" for crystal structures.
